Margin

Margin utilities are easy and versatile!

Use .m- then your size, for margin applied to all four sizes of the element.

For margin on the left and right or top and bottom, use .mx- and .my- respectively, followed by the size.

And finally, add t, l, b, or r or top, left, bottom, and right marign.


Example:

class="b-blue my-3 ml-2 mr-8"

You can also use the utility class .m-auto for automatic margin. This works for any of the prefixes listed above.
