Padding

Padding utlities are nearly identical to that of margin utilities.

Use .p- then your size, for padding applied to all four sizes of the element.

For padding on the left and right or top and bottom, use .px- and .pmy- respectively, followed by the size.

Then lastly, add t, l, b, or r or top, left, bottom, and right padding.


Example:

class="b-blue pl-6 py-3"
